Refactoring components Do and don't list

Overview

The do and don't list component could be refactored using the ticks and crosses list style and panel component, so the do and don't list code could be removed.

The do and don't list guidance can then be rewritten as a design pattern.

Styles

The ticks and crosses list style would need adding to the list styles in typography.

Patterns

Rewrite the do and don't list as a design pattern: "Help users understand more easily what they should and shouldn't do".

Example {{ panel({ "label": "Do", "HTML": "
  • cover blisters that are likely to burst with a soft plaster or dressing
  • wash your hands before touching a burst blister
  • allow the fluid in a burst blister to drain before covering it with a plaster or dressing
" }) }} {{ panel({ "label": "Don't", "HTML": "
  • do not burst a blister yourself
  • do not peel the skin off a burst blister
  • do not pick at the edges of the remaining skin
  • do not wear the shoes or use the equipment that caused your blister until it heals
" }) }}

Other design patterns could also utilise the same styles and components.
