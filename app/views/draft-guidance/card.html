Draft guidance Card

When to use a card

Use a card to provide users with a brief summary of content or a task, often with a link to more detail. Cards are frequently displayed alongside other cards to group related content or tasks.

How it works

Cards should be easy to scan for relevant and actionable information.

They can contain multiple elements, such as an image, title, text and links.

Clickable card

For cards that contain a single link, the hit area for that link can be made to wrap the entire card.

We avoid wrapping an entire card in an anchor tag as this can be a difficult experience for a screen reader user.

Card group

Multiple cards can be grouped to display a collection related topics.

You should prioritise the cards so the content users most need to know comes first.

We define the width of the cards using the grid system.
