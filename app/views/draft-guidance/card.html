When to use a card

Use a card component to provide a brief summary of content or a task, often with a link to more detail. Cards are frequently displayed alongside other cards to group related content or tasks.

Clickable card

For cards that contain a single link, the hit area for that link can be made to wrap the entire card.

We avoid wrapping an entire card in an anchor tag as this can be a difficult experience for a screen reader user.
