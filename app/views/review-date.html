Refactoring components Review date

The review date component could be removed and instead be made from existing styles and become a design pattern.

Styles

It can use the styles nhsuk-body-s, nhsuk-u-secondary-text-color and spacing overrides (if needed).

For example:

Page last reviewed: 12 February 2017
Next review due: 1 February 2020

Updated: June 2019

Components

Doesn't need a component.

Pattern

The review date component would become a pattern "Reassure users that your information is up-to-date".
